# Water Cycle Study Notes

The water cycle moves water through evaporation, condensation, precipitation, and collection.

- Evaporation changes liquid water into water vapor.
- Condensation changes water vapor into liquid droplets that form clouds.
- Precipitation includes rain, snow, sleet, and hail.
- Collection gathers water in oceans, rivers, lakes, and groundwater.

The Sun provides energy that drives evaporation. Gravity helps precipitation and runoff move water back toward collection areas.
