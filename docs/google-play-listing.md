## Full Description

```text
Flashly helps students turn learning material into interactive study cards.

Upload a PDF, image, or text file, and Flashly extracts the content, reads scanned material with OCR when needed, and generates quiz-style flashcards you can review on your phone.

Study with multiple-choice cards, track your progress, build daily learning streaks, and come back to weak cards when you need extra practice.

Flashly is built for students who want to study from lecture notes, textbook pages, exam papers, class handouts, and quick study documents without manually rewriting everything into flashcards.

Key features:
- Upload PDFs, images, and text notes
- OCR support for scanned or image-only study material
- AI-generated multiple-choice flashcards
- Review sessions with instant feedback
- XP, streaks, daily goals, and progress tracking
- Weak-card practice for targeted revision
- Clean mobile-first learning experience

Flashly is a study assistant. Always verify important academic, medical, legal, engineering, or professional information against your source material or instructor guidance.
```

## Feature Bullet Points

- Convert study files into flashcards
